Project

Blue Octane - Workshop 1

Innovation Sprint

Blue Octane is a design-led innovation workshop format I developed to rapidly explore new product opportunities and validate concepts with users. This first workshop focused on discovery and problem framing.

Workshop Format

  • Stakeholder alignment - Bring together product, engineering, design, and business stakeholders around a shared challenge
  • User research synthesis - Present existing research findings and identify knowledge gaps
  • Problem framing - Use structured exercises to define the problem space and prioritize opportunities
  • Concept generation - Rapid ideation to explore a wide range of potential solutions
  • Concept validation - Quick-turn user feedback on the most promising concepts

Outcomes

Workshop 1 established the foundation for the Blue Octane initiative - aligning the team on the problem space and generating a prioritized set of concepts to carry into the next phase of development.
