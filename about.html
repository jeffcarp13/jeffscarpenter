About Me

I'm an Innovation, Product, and Design (UX) Leader with deep experience driving business outcomes in startups and Fortune 500 companies through UX and innovative products.

For over 20 years, I've led multidisciplinary design teams - spearheading UX vision, strategy, and execution across B2C, B2B, Enterprise, SaaS, Cloud, Web, Desktop, and Mobile platforms.

My work spans diverse industries including defense (DARPA, SOCOM, US Army, US Navy), enterprise software, healthcare, and commercial technology - always with a focus on building teams, scaling design operations, and delivering measurable outcomes.

What I Do

I build UX teams and scale design operations across organizations using design-thinking methodologies and user-centered design principles. My approach combines strategic leadership with hands-on expertise to bridge the gap between business goals and user needs.
