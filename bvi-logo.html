Design

BVI Logo

Annual Sailing Trip Identity

Each year, I organize a bareboat sailing trip to the British Virgin Islands with family and friends. As a designer, I couldn't resist creating a visual identity for the trip — a logo that captures the spirit of the adventure and serves as a memento for everyone involved.

Design Brief

The logo needed to represent the BVI sailing experience: island life, trade winds, crystal waters, and the camaraderie of the crew. After the islands were devastated by hurricanes, the design also incorporated a "BVI Strong" sentiment — celebrating the resilience of the islands and the community.

A Personal Project

This is one of those projects that reminds me why I became a designer in the first place. No stakeholder reviews, no sprint planning, no KPIs — just the pure joy of creating something meaningful for the people you care about.
