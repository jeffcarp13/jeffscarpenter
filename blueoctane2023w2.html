Project

Blue Octane - Workshop 2

Prototyping & Validation

Building on the discovery work from Workshop 1, this second Blue Octane sprint focused on turning the top concepts into tangible prototypes and testing them with real users.

Workshop Format

  • Concept refinement - Narrow down the top concepts from Workshop 1 based on feasibility, viability, and desirability
  • Rapid prototyping - Build low-to-mid fidelity prototypes that capture the core experience
  • User testing - Test prototypes with target users to validate assumptions and identify gaps
  • Iteration - Refine based on user feedback within the workshop timeframe
  • Roadmap alignment - Present validated concepts to leadership with a recommended path forward

Outcomes

Workshop 2 delivered validated prototypes with user feedback, giving leadership the confidence to invest in full development. The Blue Octane format proved effective at compressing the discovery-to-validation cycle into a focused, high-energy sprint.
